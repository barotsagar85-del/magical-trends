About Magical Trends

Magical Trends is a vertically integrated natural diamond company specializing in the cutting, polishing, and global distribution of high-quality natural diamonds. With over two decades of expertise in the diamond value chain, we operate a complete supply pipeline — from rough diamond sourcing to precision polishing and international delivery.

Our cutting and polishing facilities operate under the most stringent quality standards, producing diamonds that meet and exceed GIA/IGI grading parameters. We are committed to excellence, transparency, and building long-term partnerships with discerning clients across Europe and the world.

Global Supply Chain

  • Direct rough diamond sourcing from South Africa, Botswana, Namibia & Dubai
  • End-to-end traceability from mine to market
  • State-of-the-art cutting & polishing facilities
  • Dedicated quality control with GIA-trained gemologists

Compliance & Ethics

Magical Trends maintains an unwavering commitment to ethical diamond trading:

✓ Kimberley Process (KP) Certified ✓ AML Compliant ✓ FATF Policy Adherent ✓ Zero Russian Diamonds ✓ 100% Natural Diamonds Only ✓ Full Traceability

⚠ We strictly do not deal in any Russian-origin diamonds. All rough parcels are KP certified and fully traceable to their country of origin.
